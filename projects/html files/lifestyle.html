As obvious as it is, Lionel Messi, leads a lavish life, and with $400 million net worth, who wouldn’t? Furthermore, Times Magazine tagged the Argentine as the world’s most influential people in 2011 and 2012. Furthermore, his life has been portrayed in a documentary made by filmmaker Álex de la Iglesia. Well, it was first premiered at the 2014 Venice Film Festival. As an accomplished athlete, Messi takes extra care of his body. In fact, he follows a strict diet and gym workout routine. In brief, his workout routine compromises the pillar bridge-front, lunges, hamstring stretches, and pillar skips. Additionally, he also does hurdle hop, split squats, drills, and squats.
